Your Skin has a Microbiome like Your Gut!

Your skin is home to trillions of microbes that help protect it. Over-cleansing or harsh products can throw this balance off, causing irritation and breakouts.

Dehydrated Skin and Dry Skin Are Not the Same Thing

Dry skin lacks oil, while dehydrated skin lacks water. That is why even oily skin types can feel tight or flaky, and why hydration (like hyaluronic acid) is for everyone.

Skin Repairs Itself Best at Night

Your skin goes into repair mode while you sleep. That is why a good nighttime routine is key for glowing skin. It is not just about beauty sleep, it is science.

Collagen is your skin's secret architect: until 20!

Every year after your 20s, your skin's collagen production dips by about 1%, which means less bounce and more wrinkles creeping in. Time to boost with serums!

Serums deliver powerful ingredients in tiny molecules

Because they are lightweight and packed with actives like Vitamin C or Hyaluronic Acid, serums penetrate deeper than creams for maximum impact.

Not all exfoliants are created equal

Chemical exfoliants like AHAs and BHAs gently dissolve dead skin cells, unlike physical scrubs which can cause microtears and irritation.
